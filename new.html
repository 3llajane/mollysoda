Molly Soda created a video, Who's Sorry Now, in 2017. It was a self portrait webcam performance that she created in the dark, in a hotel room. She felt the need to show this vulnerable side to her that she normally does not want people to see. Molly is used to presenting a character online, and creating an image of herself that is not completely authentic, so showing these raw emotions was important to her.
